Our Story

Goldmak was established in 1997 under the guidance of Managing Director Mr. Ramlal Rajpurohit. With over 23 years of strong presence across India, the company has grown into one of the leading names in the electrical industry.

The company is also led by CEO Mr. Sathish Singh, who focuses on building a positive and growth-oriented business environment for everyone associated with Goldmak.
